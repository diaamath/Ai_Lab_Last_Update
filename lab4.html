Training Questions

Question 1: In what historical milestone year was the landmark "Attention Is All You Need" paper published?
Correct Answer: B (2017)

Explanation: The Transformer model architecture was originally proposed by Google researchers in June 2017.

Question 2: Why do standard Transformers require explicit Positional Encodings?
Correct Answer: A

Explanation: Since the self-attention mechanism processes all tokens concurrently in parallel, it is permutation-invariant and requires added vector coordinates to capture sequence positions.

Question 3: Which trigonometric functions are leveraged in standard sinusoidal encodings?
Correct Answer: B (Sine and Cosine waves)

Explanation: Alternating sine functions map even indices while cosine functions address odd matrix element indices.
